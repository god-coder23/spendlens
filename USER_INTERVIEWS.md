## Recommended interview script

Use this to keep the conversations concrete:

1. What AI tools are you or your team currently paying for?
2. Who decided to buy each one?
3. Do you know your approximate monthly AI spend right now?
4. Where do you feel uncertain: price, plan choice, overlap, or actual ROI?
5. Have you ever downgraded, canceled, or consolidated tools? Why?
6. If a free audit tool showed you savings, what would make you trust it?
7. What would make you leave your email after seeing the results?

## What I would listen for

- Do they think in per-seat spend or total monthly bill?
- Are they more worried about wasted seats or API overuse?
- Do they want benchmarks against peers?
- Are they skeptical of vendor comparisons?
- Would they share a result page publicly, or only internally?
